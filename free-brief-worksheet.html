Free Client Brief Worksheet (The One That Actually Works)

Most client briefs fail for the same reason: they ask the client what they want instead of what they need.

The Free Worksheet

This one-page template covers five areas most freelancers skip:

  • Business objective — not "make a video," but "increase demo sign-ups from Instagram by twenty percent."
  • Audience segment — who specifically needs to see this, and what do they already believe?
  • Single message — if the viewer remembers only one thing, what is it?
  • Constraints — budget, timeline, legal restrictions, mandatory inclusions.
  • Success metric — how will we know if this worked?
